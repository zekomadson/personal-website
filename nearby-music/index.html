Proximity Music

Overview

The purpose of this page is to showcase the idea that was mentioned by Dev on 3/30/23, a new feature for Spotify or general music listeners.

The idea is that a user can opt-in to this application that will allow people near you to see and potentially hear or join the session of music that you are currently listening to.
